# 🚀 Teamwork4Freedom Wealth Calculator

Welcome to the **Teamwork4Freedom Wealth Calculator**! This powerful, browser-based tool helps you visualize exponential growth through high-frequency trading simulations, tiered dividend systems, and customizable cash flow strategies.

Whether you are modeling a personal investment strategy or planning for a team's financial freedom, this calculator provides instant, interactive insights.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 📊 **Core Calculation Engine**
- **Customizable Profit Rates:** Select between **60.0% and 65.0%** in 0.1% increments per trade.
- **High-Frequency Trading:** Simulate **1% stake trades** executed multiple times per day.
- **Tiered Dividend System:** Choose from **12 Levels** of dividend payouts (Level 1: $30 → Level 8: $20,000+), automatically applied on the **9th, 19th, and 29th** of each month.

### 💰 **Advanced Cash Flow Management**
- **External Cash Flow Controls:**
  - **One-Time Contributions/Withdrawals:** Specify exact amounts and dates.
  - **Recurring Contributions/Withdrawals:** Set custom intervals (e.g., every 30 days).
- **Dynamic Modeling:** See how adding or removing capital impacts your long-term trajectory.

### 📈 **Interactive Visual Analytics**
- **5 Professional Chart Types:**
  - **Line Graph:** Track growth trajectory over time.
  - **Stacked Area Chart:** Visualize the source of wealth (Principal vs. Profit vs. Dividends).
  - **Bar Chart:** Compare total inflows vs. outflows.
  - **Pie Chart:** Analyze the final composition of your portfolio.
  - **Waterfall Chart:** Step-by-step breakdown of value addition.
- **Fully Interactive:** Hover for exact values, click legends to toggle data, and zoom/pan for detailed analysis.

### 📱 **Universal Accessibility**
- **Single-File Design:** No installation required. Runs in any browser (Chrome, Safari, Edge, Firefox).
- **Mobile Optimized:** Works seamlessly on Android, iOS, Windows, and Linux.
- **Export Ready:** Print results, save as PDF, or export as a high-resolution JPG image.

## 🛠️ **How to Use**

1. **Start Simple:** Enter your **Starting Amount**, **Date Range**, and **Trades Per Day**.
2. **Set Your Strategy:** Select your desired **Profit Rate** (60-65%).
3. **Expand Options:** Click the **💵 External Cash Flow** or **📈 Dividend Levels** buttons to unlock advanced settings.
4. **Calculate:** Hit **🚀 Calculate Total** to see your projected wealth.
5. **Analyze:** Scroll down to view interactive graphs and export your results.
